About Chaitanya Law Chamber

The name “Chaitanya Law Chamber” was established in loving memory of the parents of Sri. Uday P. Honguntikar — Late Smt. Chitra and Late Sri. Prabhakar Honguntikar.

The Chamber is built on a legacy of integrity, dedication, and commitment to justice, proudly carried forward by their son, Sri. Uday P. Honguntikar.

Principal Advocate

Adv. Uday P. Honguntikar is a practicing advocate before the High Court of Kalaburagi Bench, District Courts, Consumer Commission, and State Commission, with experience across civil, criminal, family, and corporate matters.

Professional Highlights

  • Practice before the High Court of Kalaburagi Bench.
  • Representation in District Courts, Consumer Commission and State Commission.
  • Civil, Criminal, Family and Corporate legal matters.
  • Client-focused and result-driven legal representation.
